About Me

I'm Stellamarries Syombua, a Biostatistician and Data Analyst who enjoys making sense of data and turning it into stories that matter. Most of my work has been in public health and clinical research, where I focus on keeping data clean, applying the right methods, and helping teams understand what the numbers are really saying.

I'm especially interested in how data can guide better decisions in healthcare and beyond. Alongside my current projects, I'm learning more about machine learning, AI, survival analysis, predictive modelling, advanced statistical methods and time series analysis, skills that I believe will make my work even more practical and impactful.
